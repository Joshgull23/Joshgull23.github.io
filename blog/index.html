About Me:

Hello, I'm Josh and welcome to my website!

I guess I'll add a little bit about me.
I'm from New Plymouth and have lived there most of my life, although I have lived in Tauranga when I was 3 and Chile for a month when I was 16.

My main hobbies include coding and Inline Hockey, I started learning how to code in highschool and that's what inspired me to do the Dev Academy course. I started playing Inline Hockey when I was 7 and have played it ever since. I have represented my country twice in 2013 and 2016. In 2013 I played in the Junior Olympics and World Championships in Los Angeles for the Under 14 New Zealand Team which was amazing and in 2016 I played the same two tournaments in Hawaii for the Under 18 New Zealand Team.


I now live in Wellington with 5 other flatmates in Newtown and I am looking forward to doing the Dev Academy course and then eventually gaining a job in the tech-industry.
